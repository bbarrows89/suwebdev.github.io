A Thriving Industry Needs People Like You

The world of Web technology and Web-based applications grows dramatically every year, and has expanded from desktop computers to handheld devices, smartphones, and many other devices we use daily.

Job growth in Web Development and related fields is predicted to be 20% between 2012 and 2022, leading to thousands of new jobs being created. Currently, there are not enough Web Developers to fill all the open positions.

Retraining For Modern Technologies

In addition to those totally new to the world of Web Development, we serve students who want to improve their development skills so they can get to the next level in their career.

It's not uncommon for developers to work on a single product or project for so long that they become entrenched in those technologies. However, the outside world doesn't slow down for businesses that fail to update their tools. Developers who have been denied the opportunity to grow new skills may lose value on the job market.

Keep yourself up-to-date on the latest Frontend technologies and you will find that opportunities abound for fulfilling, well-paid work!
