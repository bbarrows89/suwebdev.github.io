A Thriving Industry Needs People Like You

The world of Web technology and Web-based applications grows dramatically every year. It began with traditional computers and laptops, but has evolved into smartphones, wearable devices, head-mounted displays, and more. The industry is growing by leaps and bounds.

Job growth in Web Development and related fields is predicted to be 20% between 2012 and 2022, leading to thousands of new jobs being created. At the same time, companies are working hard to fill the spots they already have open. There are not enough Web Developers to fill all the open positions.

Retraining For Modern Technologies

In addition to people who are totally new to the world of Web Development, we serve learners who want to improve their skills so they can get to the next-level job they deserve.

It's not uncommon to work on a single product or project for so long that you become entrenched in those technologies. However, the outside world doesn't slow down for businesses that fail to update their tools. If you have been denied the opportunity to grow new skills, then you could actually lose value on the job market.

Keep yourself up-to-date on the latest Frontend technologies and you will find that opportunities abound for fulfilling, well-paid work!
